Deal breakers before the notary

Inheritance chains, missing heirs, and old donations stop sales even when the house looks perfect. It is a document problem before it is a design problem.

In rural Siracusa and Ragusa, family shares can be split across many heirs with no single decision maker.

Documents that decide the outcome

Start from an as-built survey and compare it to the last authorised plans and the Catasto.

If they diverge, fix the chain of permits and keep the notary in the loop only after the technical checks.

Controls that keep money safe

Write conditions into the preliminary contract and link payments to verified milestones.

Use a clear scope and a bill of quantities so every party is pricing the same work.
