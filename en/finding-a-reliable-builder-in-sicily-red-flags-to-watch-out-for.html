Finding a Reliable Builder in Sicily: Red Flags to Watch Out For

Cost overruns start before the first hammer. They start in the documents. The first red flag is a quote with undefined materials.

Scope and cost control

The first red flag is a quote with undefined materials.

In coastal areas, a builder who ignores corrosion details is not qualified.

Documents that stop disputes

The computo metrico and specifications define quantities, materials, and tolerances.

If they are vague, every change becomes a negotiation.

Site supervision

Weekly site checks and photo reports keep contractors aligned and expose slippage early.

Record approvals in writing and update drawings when conditions change.
